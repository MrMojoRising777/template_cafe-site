Our History

Past

The story of our abbey dates back to the medieval ages when it was first established by a group of dedicated monks. They embarked on a journey to build a place of worship and reflection, which would later become the foundation of our rich history.

The old abbey in its original splendor during the medieval ages.

Now

Today, our abbey stands as a testament to centuries of history and transformation. While our past is deeply rooted in spirituality and contemplation, we have also embraced the art of brewing. Our brewery, nestled within the abbey grounds, combines tradition with innovation to craft exceptional beers that carry the spirit of our heritage.

The modern abbey, where tradition meets innovation in brewing.

Our state-of-the-art brewery where the magic of beer-making happens.
